Email Client - Preview

UX/UI Design

Email Client, Early Preview - UX/UI Design

Services: Experience Design, Interface Design

This is an extremely early design prototype preview for a game-changing email app. As an email Poweruser, I have yet to find an email client as simple, functional and efficient as I would like to have. That said, my goal for this project is to design an email client based on simplicity and efficiency to cater towards both power users and that older relative who has no technical ability whatsoever.

Some problems with Mobile Email include: 1 e-mail message per screen, unable to swipe from email to email, must view an email on another page (past the main home screen), and download attachments on another page. These result in unnecessary wait and loading times for the user which, in today’s world, result in much frustration.

As one may infer, the main goal of this app is to give the user all visibility and functionality needed for their emails directly from the home screen, so that taking the time to dive into each individual email is eliminated.

I've placed this project on hold indefinitely, as other projects have come up with higher priority. - TW, 03/2016
