GamerDuel

Startups Galore

Upon graduating Northeastern, I spent a couple of days at Gamerduel - a seed-funded startup located within the NorthOut incubator in Downtown Boston. Fueling my desire to submerge myself into a startup (albeit very briefly), I spent hours talking business direction and design values with the 2-man team, along with a multitude of other entrepreneurs in the incubator space. I also redesigned some UX items, including new user on boarding experience, empty challenge engagement, and visual design. I simplified their new user on boarding flow to provide a clean 3-step explanation of services and reduce overhead to joining the platform. I also drove engagement by designing a new challenge suggestion item in the challenges page if no challenges were present, rather than just showing nothing. Lastly, I gave the feed and challenge items a visual redesign to improve space efficiency and bring an overall cleaner look.
