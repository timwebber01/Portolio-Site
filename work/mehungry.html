MeHungry

Let's Eat

With some free time this summer 2016, I designed a consumer app called MeHungry to make it easier for people to find great, unique food trucks around them without any hassle of parsing through truck schedules. As an avid food truck go-er, I found myself frustrated with the food truck tracking apps on the market currently. All of those existing required too much manual information entering.
